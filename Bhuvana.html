Bhuvana

Bhuvana Chandra is an Indian lyricist, renowned for his work in the Telugu cinema.[2] He served in the Indian Air Force for 18 years before turning into a lyricist.[3] He penned lyrics for more than 2000 songs.[4] He also wrote more than 100 stories, a novel, and many articles in magazines. He participated as a judge in some reality shows. He also acted in the film Welcome Obama directed by Singeetam Srinivasa Rao.
